E-Commerce

E-commerce is the latest trend of business process favored by majority, it is the online business done through the Internet. E commerce may also relate to electronic data interchange where data from the computer of one company is transferred to another company's computer. With the IT revolution across the globe since the early last decade, ecommerce has become the latest trend of business process favored by majority. E-commerce is mainly categorized in three division like B2B, B2C and C2C i.e., business to business, business to customer and customer to customer.E-commerce application is all about, creating dynamic web pages that are suitable enough for commercial purpose by adding features like shopping cart, pay pal and other attractive features like site's newsletter that updates registered members about the latest ecommerce development, discounts around the marketplace etc. so that customers remain hooked up in your website and get maximum possible exposure to the new services and products in the website resulting more sale.

The main basic e-commerce requirements are:
Shopping cart creation - A very important and crucial part of online shopping website which ensures a live shopping experience, since it deals with payment and security, a secured order panel and processing system is vital to the running of your business. It is a window to our customer where he can view the details of the product like:manufacturing & expiry dates, price etc.

Payment Gateway Integration - The next critical part is the integration of Payment Gateway Service Provider who has the authorization for accepting credit card on behalf of the merchant. This can be any Bank or Authorized Company which is allowed to accept and process the credit cards online. For enabling secure transactions , providers are certified by Digital Certified Authorities like Verisign, Pay systems etc. which build trust among buyer and can transact safely.

To approach the Payment Gateway Service Provider - With this step, we approach the completion of your e-presence. Xenial team has the ability to well integrate the Payment Gateway software with your website to carry online transaction in a safe and secure manner.

Usability - We design best user interfaces for better customer interaction for the optimize use efficient shopping carts with outstanding funtional efficiency. We understand the importance of having an online shopping cart that is easy to operate and is user-friendly.

Management - From updating your carts, your product details, prices and catalogues quickly and easily. Shopping carts developed by us is so easy to manage that you yourself feel the art of interactive screens that even your non-technical employees can handle it smoothly.
